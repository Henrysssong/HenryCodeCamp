HenryCodeCamp Syllabus

1. Overview

Welcome to HenryCodeCamp! This three-month long course is designed for absolute beginners who want to become software engineers. Throughout the course, you will learn the fundamentals of computer science and gain hands-on experience with various tech stacks.

2. Prerequisites

There are no specific prerequisites for this course. It is open to anyone with a passion for learning and a desire to become a software engineer.

3. Course Format

The course will be conducted through a combination of lectures, hands-on coding exercises, and projects. Each week, you will have a set of topics to cover and assignments to complete. You will also have access to online resources and a dedicated Slack channel for discussions and support.

4. Appointment with Henry

As part of the course, you will have the opportunity to schedule one-on-one appointments with Henry, our experienced software engineer and instructor. These appointments are designed to provide personalized guidance and support to help you succeed in the course.

5. Grading

The course will be graded based on your performance in assignments, projects, and exams. You will receive regular feedback on your progress and have the opportunity to improve your skills throughout the course.
